    The Outlier is an icebreaking game for 3-8 players to guess who is lacking a bit of crucial information

    Rules:

    1. 1. Enter your names. Names must be different from each other and not empty.
    2. 2. Every player looks at the secret word (or do they?)
    3. 3. The outlier doesn't actually see the word and has to pretend they have seen it.
    4. 4. Players take it in turns to ask eachother questions based on the word.
    5. 5. At the end of the question round, players vote on who they think the outlier was
    6. 6. If the outlier isnt picked or there is a tie on the voting, the outlier wins!
